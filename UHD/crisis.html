WHAT TO DO IF YOU ARE IN CRISIS

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call 911. An individual experiencing a crisis during our office hours is encouraged to come to UHD Student Counseling as soon as possible and ask to speak with a Counselor. In the event of an after-hours crisis, please call 911, go to your nearest local emergency room, or call (713) 500-3852 and ask to be transferred to the “On Call Counselor"
