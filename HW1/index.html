About Me

I'm a 36-year old immigrant and a computer scientist. While I have a deep knowledge of Java, C and C++, web development is my current area of focus. I have utilized numerous web-based languages and tools including: HTML, CSS, jQuery, Javascript, PHP, Perl, MySQL, Bootstrap, Foundation, WordPress, Joomla, dotCMS and Weebly.

for 15 years after graduating from the University of Houston with a Bachelors in Computer Science I ran an online-business which required me to create and maintain my site while also manage the day-to-day business. I recently closed my business down in order to pursue a full-time career as a full-stack web developer as it provides me an unrelenting source of intellectual and artistic fulfillment.
